About Our Mission

Connecting surplus food with those in need while reducing environmental impact

Our Story

Founded in 2020, our platform emerged from a simple observation: massive food waste coexisting with widespread hunger.

Our founders, a group of environmental scientists and software developers, recognized the need for a technological solution that could bridge the gap between food donors and recipients while ensuring proper food safety protocols.

What started as a local initiative in one community has now grown into a nationwide network of food donors, recipients, and waste management professionals working together to create a more sustainable food ecosystem.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We believe that no one should go hungry when there is surplus food available. Our work is driven by empathy for those facing food insecurity.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing the environmental impact of food waste through responsible donation, composting, and recycling practices.

Safety & Quality

We maintain the highest standards of food safety, ensuring that all donated food meets quality requirements and is handled properly.

Collaboration

We believe that solving complex problems requires partnership between businesses, nonprofits, governments, and communities.

Innovation

We continuously seek new technologies and approaches to improve the efficiency and impact of food recovery and waste reduction.

Transparency

We operate with openness about our processes, impacts, and challenges, building trust with all stakeholders.
